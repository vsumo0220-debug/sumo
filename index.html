Core Technology

Engineered for your lifestyle

Six breakthrough features that transform how you think about staying hydrated.

Smart Hydration Tracking

Built-in sensors monitor your exact water intake throughout the day. Set personalized goals and hit them every time.

Temperature Display

Real-time LED display shows your drink's exact temperature — always know if your coffee is too hot or your water perfectly cold.

Reminder Notifications

Intelligent reminders buzz your wrist when it's time to hydrate — customizable intervals that fit your schedule and activity.

Mobile App Sync

Syncs with iOS and Android over Bluetooth. View trends, adjust settings, and export data to Apple Health or Google Fit.

Leak-Proof Design

Triple-sealed lid with magnetic lock mechanism — toss it in your bag worry-free. IPX7 rated, fully submersible up to 1 metre.

24-Hour Battery

A single USB-C charge powers all smart features for a full day. Wireless charging dock available separately.

Born from frustration.
